📔 Family Diary

Free, shared family notepad. Deploy in 5 minutes.

What is Family Diary?
A simple, free app where your family can share notes, messages, and reminders. Everyone gets their own color. No ads, no tracking, no cost.

Getting Started (5 minutes)

1

Download the app

You only need ONE file: index.html

2

Sign up for Netlify (free)

Create a free account to host your app on the web. Email or Google sign-in.

3

Deploy to Netlify

Drag the index.html file you downloaded onto Netlify.

4

Create your Firebase project (5 min)

When your app loads, the setup wizard will guide you through creating a free Firebase project. Just follow the steps.

5

Share with your family

Netlify gives you a URL. Share it with family members. They open the link and start using it immediately.

Questions?

❓ Do I need a GitHub account?

No. You only need Netlify (free) and Firebase (free). GitHub is just where the code lives.

❓ Does it cost anything?

No. Netlify and Firebase both have generous free plans that cover family use. No credit card required.

❓ Is my data private?

Yes. Your family gets its own Firebase project. Only people with your app's URL can see the messages. You control the data.

❓ Can I customize it?

Yes. The code is open-source. You can modify colors, text, features — whatever you want.

❓ I got stuck. Where's help?

The setup wizard walks you through everything. If you're stuck, the detailed Deployment Guide has screenshots and troubleshooting.
